VARIETIES

Alphonso Mango

Known as the "King of Mangoes," Alphonso is prized for its rich, buttery texture and exquisite sweetness. Grown primarily in Ratnagiri, Maharashtra, it has a distinct aroma and a vibrant golden-yellow hue, making it a sought-after variety both nationally and internationally.

Kesar Mango

Hailing from Gujarat, the Kesar mango is renowned for its unique saffron-infused flavor and vibrant orange color. With a balanced sweetness and a melting texture, it is a favorite for desserts and culinary delights.

Langra Mango

Originating from Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, the Langra mango is valued for its greenish-yellow skin and fibrous, tangy flesh. Despite its irregular shape, its distinct taste and aroma make it a popular choice.

Dasheri Mango

Grown in Malihabad, Uttar Pradesh, the Dasheri mango is recognized for its sweet aroma and smooth, non-fibrous texture. With a medium size and a yellowish-green skin, it is a preferred variety for its delectable taste.

Totapuri Mango

Predominantly found in Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, and Tamil Nadu, the Totapuri mango is characterized by its distinct beak-like shape. This variety is favored for its tangy taste and is commonly used in pickles and culinary preparations.

Neelam Mango

Originating from Andhra Pradesh, the Neelam mango is known for its vibrant red blush on a yellow skin. It has a sweet and mildly tangy flavor, with a firm texture. Often enjoyed as a dessert mango, it is a popular choice during the summer season.
